About Us

Sustainable Futures Initiative (SFI) is dedicated to promoting sustainable development in Tanzania. We tackle key challenges like gender inequality, climate change, food insecurity, environmental degradation, and more. We contribute to the SDGs through advocacy, capacity building, community engagement, and knowledge-sharing.

Our Vision

To support Tanzania in achieving the UN Sustainable Development Goals through innovative and sustainable solutions that foster economic growth, climate resilience, and inclusion.

Our Mission

To create a Tanzania where everyone has access to basic needs, opportunities, and a dignified, sustainable life.

Our Focus Sectors

  • Ecosystems and Biodiversity
  • Climate Change
  • Health, Water, and Food Safety
  • Forestry and Agriculture
  • Education
  • Gender Equality
  • Sustainable Cities
  • Water and Sanitation

Our Programs

We foster inclusive participation through education, CSR, communication strategies, events, workshops, and volunteer support.

Research & Advocacy

  • Research on mitigation, adaptation, and loss & damage
  • Policy analysis and advocacy campaigns
  • Collaborations with research institutions
  • Dissemination of findings

Capacity Building

  • Training workshops and mentorship
  • Best practice sharing

Climate Action

We implement mitigation and adaptation projects with communities to build resilience and reduce emissions.

Water & Sanitation

We provide WASH services, sanitation infrastructure, and promote hygiene practices in underserved communities.

Gender Equality & Education

We empower women and ensure equitable, quality education for all, especially in marginalized areas.

Policy Advocacy & Engagement

We influence climate-responsive policies through partnerships with government, civil society, and global institutions.
